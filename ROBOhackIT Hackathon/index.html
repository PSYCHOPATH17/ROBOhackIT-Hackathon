Our Mission

Our incentive is to instill a change in the lives of those that suffer from poverty every day. The organization name, Impoverish Poverty, gets its name from the idea of impoverishing, or stripping, poverty itself of its valuables: the poor. Like a table without legs, poverty cannot stand without the impoverished people. To effectively inaugurate this mission globally, we will be utilizing our 4-step signature plan, consisting of:

Create a Plan

Facts

Poverty is a striking issue in the modern world that has been around since long before. Many individuals and families are battling it every single second and their lives tougher than most can imagine. Here are some facts about poverty to fill you in:

Fact One:

Almost half of the children in the world live in impoverished conditions.

Fact Two:

About 71% of the population is impoverished.

Fact Three:

Close to 22,000 kids die every day in impoverished homes.

Fact Four:

Over half of Americans are believed to be living below the poverty line.

Fact Five:

There are five countries that house over 3/5 of the world's impoverished: India, Bangladesh, China, Nigeria, and The Democratic Republic of the Congo.
